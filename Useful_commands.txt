# 3. Download group of files from hpc to locally 

### OPTION 1 ###

## a. Group and compress files (on HPC)
tar -czvf all_counts.tar.gz */*_counts.tsv # i.e. all files ending with "_counts.tsv"

## b. Download them from HPC to local (local)
scp vsc48905@login.hpc.ugent.be:/data/gent/vo/000/gvo00082/vsc48905/Project/Preprocessing/Yang/Nextflow/Output/results/isoquant/isoquant_out/all_counts.tar.gz ./

## c. Decompress files (local)
tar -xzvf all_counts.tar.gz # IN INPUT DIRECTORY!
